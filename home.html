|| Drunk Trivia ||

Confused?

Well, let me explain. Drunk trivia is a drinking mini-game that proves you with a drink to make at the beginning of the game. After that, you will be given a series of questions if you get the question right then you will be rewarded with 100 points. If you get the question wrong then bottoms up (that means you drink). Now go ahead and see how good of a tipsy trivia master you are!
